DELIVERY INFORMATION

There are many factors that determine the delivery time, such as your location, order quantity and product type.

- Your Location: the remote areas take longer to arrive.

- Order Quantity: Large quantities may be taking time to prepare for shipment.

- Product Type: Cake tools and materials ship faster compared to birthday decorations, there are some of them that need time to make them by order.

Estimated shipping time: 3-7 business days It's usually taking less if you in Cairo. We are working hard to deliver your order as quickly as possible. If you are in a rush and need your order quickly, contact us via Facebook or online chat and we will help you.

There are a number of reasons for a delayed delivery:

  • 1- The address is not clear in detail or the address is wrong.
  • 2- The contact information is wrong.
  • 3- Not finding you in the previously specified time to receive your order.

We fully understand that you need to know when to expect delivery and don't want to sit at home all day waiting on a van to arrive, so our delivery partner will keep in contact with you from the moment they collect your order from Towipi right up to the point of delivery and you can track your order also.

RETURNS POLICY

Yes, you can cancel your order from your account before it has been shipped. - Please note: customized orders cannot be canceled.

CAN I CANCEL AN ORDER AFTER IT HAS BEEN SHIPPED?

Yes, you can cancel it and only bank incurred fees will be deducted from the amount paid. - Please note: customized orders cannot be canceled.

You are responsible for all orders placed on your account, including for any mistakes in your order caused by you. If you notice this mistake and would like to edit your order before shipping, contact us to help you.

We can refund or exchange goods in the following condition:

- Defected or in unacceptable conditions.

- Not used (with original tags and/or packaging).

- Towipi doesn't offer a refund for Delivery Service.

- We accept returns matching the above conditions only within 14 days of purchasing date.

- The package have to be in an original status to be able to return it with the above conditions.

- No Exchange and No Refund on customized order and cake tools used or its packaging damaged.
